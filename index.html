Welcome to CocoaHeadsFFM!

We are the Frankfurt chapter of Cocoaheads. We meet once every month and talk about iOS and OS X development. If you're into Cocoa and in Frankfurt (or in the Rhein/Main area), come in and say hi.

Follow us on Twitter: @cocoaheadsffm and join our Facebook Group or add pull requests for this site at github cocoaheadsffm.

Usually we meet every first Tuesday (Monday) of each month.
